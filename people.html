Professor

Jiyun Lee

Associate Professor · KAIST Aerospace Engineering

Prof. Jiyun Lee leads the GNSS Lab at KAIST, where her research focuses on GNSS integrity monitoring, satellite-based augmentation systems, ionospheric modeling for the Korean Positioning System, and GNSS radio occultation for atmospheric remote sensing.
