01 — 01

AI Safety (Vision AI)

As AI-driven perception systems become integral to autonomous navigation, ensuring their reliability and safety is critical. We investigate integrity monitoring frameworks for Vision AI pipelines — including camera-based localization and object detection — developing fault detection and alert mechanisms that provide quantifiable safety guarantees for aviation and autonomous vehicle applications.

01 — 02

GNSS Augmentation System

Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS) and Ground-Based Augmentation Systems (GBAS) broadcast differential corrections and integrity bounding data to support precision navigation. We develop augmentation system architectures for the Korean Positioning System (KPS), targeting compliance with ICAO standards for precision approach, landing, and urban air mobility operations.

Related Papers

JPNT · 2022

01 — 03

Integrity for Multi-Sensor Navigation Systems

Modern navigation platforms fuse GNSS with inertial measurement units (IMU), cameras, LiDAR, and other sensors to maintain continuous positioning in challenging environments. We develop integrity monitoring methods for tightly-coupled multi-sensor fusion systems, extending RAIM concepts to heterogeneous sensor architectures for autonomous vehicles and UAM platforms.
