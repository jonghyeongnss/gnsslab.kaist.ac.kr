Research Areas

Our Work

We develop algorithms and systems at the intersection of GNSS signal processing, atmospheric remote sensing, and satellite navigation.

View All Research →

01

🌐

GNSS Radio Occultation

Retrieving atmospheric profiles from bending angle measurements using phase matching, Abel inversion, and wavelet-based signal processing for the lower troposphere.

→

02

📡

Satellite Navigation & Positioning

Developing integrity monitoring, error correction, and positioning algorithms for GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and the Korean Positioning System (KPS).

→

03

⚡

Ionospheric Modeling

Characterizing and correcting ionospheric delays for single- and dual-frequency GNSS receivers, with applications to aviation safety and precision navigation.
