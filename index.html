Research Areas

Our Work

We develop algorithms and systems at the intersection of GNSS signal processing, atmospheric remote sensing, and satellite navigation.

View All Research →

01

GNSS Integrity

Developing integrity monitoring and fault detection algorithms to ensure the safety and reliability of GNSS-based navigation for aviation and critical applications.

→

02

LEO PNT

Leveraging Low Earth Orbit satellite signals for positioning, navigation, and timing — enabling resilient alternatives to traditional GNSS in challenging environments.

→

03

Remote Sensing

Using GNSS radio occultation and reflectometry to retrieve atmospheric and surface parameters — from tropospheric bending angles to soil moisture and sea surface height.
